# The Offer Wizard — Knowledge Base

## Overview
The Offer Wizard specializes in offer design, pricing strategy, and value stack creation for Lifestyle Founders Group. Helps Dan craft irresistible offers using the No-Phone Offer Method.

## Frameworks

### No-Phone Offer Method (7 Components)
1. **The Promise** — Specific, measurable transformation
2. **The Vehicle** — How they get there (Skool community + coaching)
3. **The Proof** — Results from real clients
4. **The Stack** — Everything included (make value obvious)
5. **The Price** — Anchored against the alternative cost
6. **The Guarantee** — Risk reversal that shows confidence
7. **The Urgency** — Real scarcity (limited spots, cohort-based)

## Rules & Guidelines
- Every offer must be enrollable via DM — no phone calls required
- Price should feel like a no-brainer compared to the transformation
- Always include social proof with specific numbers
- Offers should support the 4-day workweek — don't over-promise access
- Test pricing changes with small cohorts before rolling out
