## PATH A: BUILD A NEW OFFER (Harrison x Hormozi 9-Prompt Framework)

### WHO:

**Prompt 1: Market Research**
Imagine you are the TARGET MARKET. Describe: overview of yourself, motivations (goals and aspirations), perceived obstacles and fears, core values, inner dialogues, competition, ultimate dream outcomes. Obstacles just before and just after attaining dream outcomes.

Psychographics: 6 interests, 6 experts they follow, 6 books, 6 tools, 6 publications, 6 groups, 6 products, Top 3 social media platforms.

**Prompt 2: Client Boundaries**
5 types of people/traits to ACCEPT as clients. 5 to REJECT.

### WHAT:

**Prompt 3: Core Currencies**
- CORE Currency — The primary "business I'm in"
- 10 tangible currencies the product INCREASES
- 10 currencies the product DECREASES

**Prompt 4: Problems & Solutions**
10 potential problems the TARGET MARKET would perceive in pursuing their desired outcome. Possible solutions for each. Bold the 3 primary problems they'd pay a premium to solve.

**Prompt 5: The Million Dollar Message**
Framework: "I Help [TARGET MARKET] achieve [CORE CURRENCY] in [SPECIFIC TIMELINE] so they can [DREAM OUTCOME] without [PROBLEM]"

### HOW:

**Prompt 6: Offer Creation**
Rewrite obstacles as solutions: "[AWESOME UNIQUE FRAMEWORK NAME]: How to [YAY] without [BOO] even if you [GREATEST OBSTACLE]."

Focus on: massive pain, available purchasing power, easy to target, growing market.

Unpack each step into 3 micro steps for 9 total. Phrase each as their own offers.

**Hormozi Value Equation:** Value = (Dream Outcome x Perceived Likelihood of Achievement) / (Time Delay x Effort and Sacrifice)

**Prompt 7: Expand & Enhance**
- 3-5 sub-offers for the most important steps
- Scarcity (limited supply, seats, bonuses, never available again)
- Urgency (rolling cohorts, seasonal, ticking clock)
- Guarantee that reverses risk: "If you do not get X result in Y time, we will Z"

**Prompt 8: Bonuses**
5 bonuses. Quantify dollar value of each. Total bonus value > 2x the offer price.

### WHY:

**Prompt 9: Campaign**
- Type of product, Price, Delivery timeline, Name
- Target Avatars (min 3), Ideal client traits (min 3)
- 5 Currencies, Core Currency
- Customer ultimate outcome
- Main struggles/obstacles (min 3)
- Skills clients need (min 3), Beliefs to adopt (min 3), Character traits to build (min 3)
- Features (min 3), Benefits (min 3), Deliverables (min 3)

## PATH B: REFINE AN EXISTING OFFER

### Step 1: Input Current Offer
Paste brief description, sales page, or Notion doc.

### Step 2: Soul-Aligned Offer Clarity Check (5 YES/NO)
1. Do I love working with this type of client?
2. Am I an expert at solving their problems?
3. Is this offer profitable — can they pay premium pricing?
4. Can I easily find these people online?
5. Does this align with a deeper purpose or calling?

If any is NO → flag for refinement.

### Step 3: Sharpening Prompts
- What is the single biggest result you deliver?
- What is the real problem you solve (in client's words)?
- Why is now the right time to solve it?
- What's one simple framework you walk clients through?
- Any unnecessary steps, fluff, or bloated promises?

### Step 4: Apply Value Equation
- Can we increase perceived likelihood of success?
- Can we shorten time to result?
- Can we reduce client's effort?
- Can we amplify the dream outcome?

### Step 5: Reframe with Million Dollar Message
Generate 2-3 variations in different tones (bold, playful, luxurious, grounded, spiritual).

## PATH C: BREAK INTO MINI PRODUCTS & LEAD MAGNETS

### Step 1: Dissect Offer into 3 Parts
- 3 Pillars of transformation
- 3 Currencies it increases
- 3 Major Struggles it solves

### Step 2: Generate Lead Magnets
"What's one thing your client needs to understand, believe, or do before they say yes?"
- 1-Page Checklist
- Mini Video Training
- "Top 3 Mistakes" Carousel
- DM Script or Pre-Call Framework

### Step 3: Build Micro Offers ($27-$297)
"What's the fastest win or micro-transformation you can deliver in 1-2 weeks?"
- Deep dive mini-course
- Paid workshop
- Done-with-you sprint
- 7-day challenge

Each should: be 10% of main offer's value, solve one clear painful problem, naturally lead to core offer.

### Step 4: Workshop Outline
"[Workshop Name]: How to [CORE RESULT] without [BIG OBSTACLE] — even if [COMMON FEAR]"
1. Pain intro → 2. Belief shift → 3. Roadmap/3-step system → 4. CTA to main offer

### Step 5: DM/Ad Hook
Story-based hook tying micro-offer back to brand. Used for: story slides, Reels, paid ads, "comment below" posts.

## PATH D: IMPROVE DELIVERY SYSTEMS & CLIENT SUCCESS

### Step 1: Define Core Transformation
Point A → Point B. Example: "Burnt-out therapist → $10k/mo online wellness coach"

### Step 2: Identify 3 Core Pillars
Main themes or milestones. Example: Self-Leadership, Signature Offer Creation, Client Acquisition.

### Step 3: 3x3 Rule — 9-Step Transformation Path
Break each pillar into 3 micro-steps. Each should feel small, actionable, rewarding.

### Step 4: Quick Win Timeline
- **1 Minute:** Affirmation/relief they made the right choice
- **1 Hour:** First action taken
- **1 Day:** Micro-result or mini breakthrough
- **1 Week:** Momentum milestone

### Step 5: Delivery Structure
- Current delivery methods (Calls, Voxer, LMS, etc.)
- How do clients get help?
- Where do clients fall off or get stuck?

### Step 6: Delivery Blueprint Output
- Core Transformation (A → B)
- 3x3 Framework
- Weekly cadence
- Resource stack
- Delivery checklist per milestone
- Onboarding sequence with quick wins

### Step 7: Rules of Engagement
- Boundaries of support
- Rules to stay "in momentum"
- Clear Day 1 expectations

## PATH E: CREATE MARKETING & MESSAGING

### Step 1: Million Dollar Message
Generate 3-5 variations: Bold & direct, Heart-led, Premium, Disruptive, Story-based.

### Step 2: Psychological Copywriting Layer

**Core Emotional Triggers:** Hope, Relief, Belonging, Aspiration, Fear, Pride, Power, Gratitude

**Cognitive Biases:** Loss Aversion, Social Proof, Status/Authority Bias, Contrast Effect, Anchoring

**By Market Temperature:**
- Cold: Curiosity, identity collapse, pain contrast
- Warm: Safety, future pacing, mechanism
- Hot: Urgency, ROI, believability, status CTA

### Step 3: Messaging Outputs
- 3 DM Hooks / Reels Intros
- 1 Short-Form Story CTA: "DM 'X' to see how this works"
- 2-Paragraph 'About' Section
- 3 Email Subject Lines + Openers

### Step 4: CTA Matrix
| Stage | Temp | Style | Example |
|---|---|---|---|
| TOF | Cold | Curiosity pull | "DM me 'MAP' if you want to see how this works." |
| MOF | Warm | Mechanism insight | "Want to see how Sarah flipped her biz in 30 days?" |
| BOF | Hot | Permissioned CTA | "Only if you're ready. Here's the next step." |
