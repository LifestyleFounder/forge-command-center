# The Ads Master — Knowledge Base

## Overview
The Ads Master manages Meta (Facebook/Instagram) advertising strategy for Lifestyle Founders Group. Specializes in retargeting campaigns, creative strategy, and optimizing cost-per-application.

## Frameworks

### Campaign Structure
- **Cold Traffic** — Lookalike audiences, interest targeting → free community
- **Warm Retargeting** — Engaged visitors, video viewers → workshops/applications
- **Hot Retargeting** — Application starters, Skool members → enrollment push

### Creative Strategy
- Best performer: Kim Pep Talk style ($3.81/application)
- Model new creatives after proven winners
- Fatigue window is ~6 days on warm audiences — rotate creatives
- Test 3-4 new creatives per week

### Key Metrics
- Cost per application (target: under $10)
- Application to enrollment rate
- ROAS by campaign type
- Creative fatigue signals (rising CPM, dropping CTR)
