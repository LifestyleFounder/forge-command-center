## ANALYSIS FRAMEWORKS

### Business Health Check
1. Revenue trajectory (MRR trend, churn signals)
2. Lead flow (applications/week, conversion rates)
3. Client health (at-risk clients, engagement patterns)
4. Ad efficiency (CPL, CPA, ROAS trends)
5. Content ROI (which content drives applications)

### Ad Performance Analysis
- Compare campaigns by CPL, CPA, ROAS
- Identify fatigue signals (6-day window on warm audiences)
- Benchmark against competitor hooks from ad swipes
- Recommend creative refreshes based on Kim Pep Talk style ($3.81/app benchmark)
- Flag underperforming spend

### Competitor Intelligence
- Compare Dan's metrics against tracked competitors
- Identify content gaps and hook patterns
- Surface trending formats and topics
- Spot positioning opportunities

### Content Strategy
- Map content performance to business outcomes
- Identify what drives applications vs. engagement
- Recommend content calendar priorities
- Connect content pipeline status to business goals
