## PART 1: MASTER SCRIPT FLOW (ECAFI™)

### I. ENGAGE & CONNECT (The Opener)

**Goal:** Gauge temperature, move from passive to active.

**"This or That" Opener:**
- "Thanks for the follow! Are you here for the vids or looking to get into shape?"
- "Do you focus more on nutrition or lifting?"
- "Do you prefer working online or in person?"

**"Story" Opener (Money Maker):**
- "So tell me a bit about you, what's your story and what got you into [Topic]?"
- Logic: Long response = Hot Lead. One-word = Cold Lead (lean out / send free value)

**New Follower Opener:**
- "[Name]! Appreciate the [action]. [Personalized comment]. What's up — you here for the vids or looking to [outcome]?"

**New Skool Member:**
- "Hey [Name], Welcome to [Community] — when you joined you mentioned [entry question answer]..."

**Re-engagement:**
- "Hey [Name]! Just saw you pop up on my feed, prompted me to reach out"
- "I noticed you've been in our group — did you happen to see [resource]? Interested?"

**Value Hook Opener:**
- "Hey [Name]! I just saw your comment on my recent post about ___. Here is the link to check it out. [relevant follow-up question]"

**Pivot (Permission — REQUIRED before assessment):**
- "Mind if I ask a couple of questions to see if I can help?"

**Core Principle:** Reward/praise the client for answering. Leave emojis on responses that move closer to the sale.

**Praise Lines:**
- "Appreciate that [Name]"
- "Got it" / "100%" / "Love it"
- "I hear you"
- "Struggle is real…"
- "Sounds like you've been busy..."
- "What I've found is..."

---

### II. ASSESS (The Diagnosis / A-to-B Gap)

**Point A (Current State):**
- "What does your revenue/health look like right now?"
- "What have you tried so far?"
- "How long you been at this?"

**Point B (Desired State):**
- "Where would you like to be in 12 months?"
- "What's the big goal with it?"
- "What kind of lifestyle would that allow?"
- "I need specifics"

**Gap (Pain):**
- "What's stopping you from getting there?"
- "How long have you been struggling with this?"
- "What are 2-3 things missing so you can get to [goal]?"
- "What do you need most so you're able to get to [outcome]?"
- "What's the top 3 things getting in the way?"
- "How will you feel if you're in the same place a year from now?"

**Mirror (Validation):**
- "Oh, you've been struggling for 5 years? Damn, that's a long time."
- "I get that… better health doesn't have to mean more hours"
- "Uh oh..."
- "Oh no..."

---

### III. FRAME (The Expert Stance)

**Buying Zone Reframes:**
- Hope: "There are 22M millionaires. Think you could be one?"
- Confidence: "To hit your goal, you only need 5 more units/month."
- Urgency: "Wouldn't it be weird if an Olympian didn't have a coach?"

**Authority Lines:**
- "Totally. Those problems are my jam."
- "These are areas I explicitly work on with coaching clients"
- "Working with some of the best in the [industry] space"
- "Last year on average I helped my clients [specific result]"
- "Unpacking how I [relevant achievement]"
- "Small curated group by invite only"

---

### IV. INVITE (The Close)

**Setup:**
- "Would you like some help with that?"
- "I've got all the details in a video/PDF. Mind if I send it here?"
- "Do you want help so you can [outcome]?"

**Testimonial First:** Send testimonial before the offer. Wait a few minutes.

**Commitment Test:**
- "On a scale of 1-10, how important is this to you?"
- "Is this a now or a later thing?"
- "Is this a NICE to have or a MUST have?"
- "I only work with action-takers. Are you ready to commit?"

**Drop the Offer (Chat):**
- "Ok great! Sounds like this could be a fit. I have all the details on a Notion Doc I can drop right here. You can let me know if it feels like a hell yes or a hell no or if you have any questions — Cool?"
- Wait for response before dropping the offer.
- "Do you have 5 minutes right now?"

**Drop the Offer (Call):**
- "So the next step would be a quick 15 minute game plan where I can share [something relevant] and see if I can help. Would that be helpful?"
- "I'll leave my chat open — let me know once you booked so I can make sure it shows up."

---

### V. TIE-DOWN & ONBOARDING

- "Awesome! What stood out the most for you?"
- "Send me a screenshot of the cute dog on the thank you page."
- Post-close: "Super pumped to work with you!! How ya feeling? Nervous? Excited? Ready?"

Post-enrollment:
- "Calls are every Wednesday"
- "Lock in your calendar"
- "Get into the app and complete the onboarding checklist"
- "Just show up to weekly calls"
- "If you commit I'll make sure you don't fail"

## PART 2: OBJECTION HANDLING LIBRARY

### "Too Expensive / Can't Afford"
1. "Totally get it. Finances aside, what do you think about the program?"
2. (They say they love it)
3. "If I helped you make $2k in 30 days, would it be worth it?"

### "No Time"
1. "Totally get it! You want to make sure you have time to implement"
2. "Some of our best clients see results with as little as 2 hours a week"
3. "Do you think you could find 2 hours to [get to your goal]?"
4. If yes: "One of my mentors said 'If you don't sacrifice for what you want, then what you want becomes the sacrifice'"
5. "Are you willing to sacrifice a bit of leisure to [outcome]?"

### "DIY / Want to Do It Myself"
1. "Totally get that! And I respect your hustle. Is that the only thing holding you back?"
2. "Let me ask you a question... Is that what you'd advise your potential clients to do?"
3. "Would you rather spend 3-6 months trying to figure it out on your own? Or start with me today and get results in the first 30 days?"

### "Start Next Week / Think About It"
1. "Amazing! I love it! What's holding you back from getting started today?"
2. "The truth is that 99% of the time when people say 'next week' it really means 'never.'"
3. "As a coach if I let you off the hook, I let you down. Don't you think it's time to fully commit?"

### "Too Long a Commitment"
1. "Totally understand! Is the commitment the only thing holding you back?"
2. "Start with the monthly and I will let you upgrade after we get some quick wins."
3. "And if you're not satisfied, you can just cancel no questions asked"

### "Pay Later from Profits"
- "I have to honour my other clients' investments + when you pay you pay attention!!"

## PART 3: GHOST PROTOCOL (Follow-Up Sequencing)

**Step 1 (30 min):** Go into the conversation and LIKE one of their messages or genuinely engage with their profile.

**Step 2 (60 min):** Ping them:
- "Get my note?"
- "Ping ;)"
- "[Name]?"
- "Sorry I got busy" (play dumb)
- "Sorry if I offended..."
- A well-placed GIF

**Step 3 (Same day):** No more follow-ups that day.

**Step 4 (Next day):** "Morning!"

**Then:** 3 days, 7 days, 14 days, 21 days

**Core Principle:** Follow up shamelessly and relentlessly. Time kills momentum. Best conversations happen start to finish in a single day.

## PART 4: PSYCHOLOGY & GOD MODE

### God Mode
- High status. No begging. No chasing.
- "If I can't help you, I don't sell you."
- High presence, high integrity, non-needy.

### Lean Out Rule (Match Disinterest with Dismissal)
- "All good dude. Circle back when you want to move faster."
- "Can only help people swimming toward me"
- "Only working with the doers"
- "Here when you're ready to level up"
- "Typically only work with those who are 110% committed to their vision"

---

## PART 5: CHAT ETIQUETTE RULES

- Short, direct. Like a high schooler.
- Use emojis. End every message with a question.
- Never send long paragraphs.
- Never write like ChatGPT. Coach like a real person.
- Make one clear point at a time.
- React with emojis on messages that move toward the sale.
- WE SEEK TO UNDERSTAND RATHER THAN SELL THEM.

## PART 6: TEAM & PROCESS LOGIC

### Opener vs Closer Roles
- **Opener:** Engage + Connect (warm up, qualify, find the tip of the iceberg)
- **Closer:** Assess + Frame + Invite (dig deeper, establish authority, close)
- **Handoff:** Only after opener confirms qualification, relationship, and at least the tip of the pain point.

### Engage: Sourcing Opportunities
1. New followers/connections
2. Story views
3. Comments
4. Likes
5. Inbox Messages
6. Mentions or Tags

### Pre-Qualify Checklist
- Determine if the lead is a potential fit
- Avoid opening inactive accounts
- Check social bio for key word / identifier
- Look for business links
- Google their name
- Check BackPak for the Lead

### Processing Order (Bottom-Up)
1. Offers Made (close these first)
2. Qualified Leads
3. New Opens

### 5-Minute Rule
Respond within 5 minutes of interest to keep 90% chance of close.

### Minimum Message Count
A chat should have at least 9 messages before moving to the solution or offer.

### Momentum Questions (Create 5-8 go-to Q's)
- "Do you work mostly with clients 1:1 or in groups?"
- "Are you more interested in personal development or relationship work?"
- "Do you prefer working online or in person?"
- "Are you more focused on nutrition or lifting?"

### Pre-Assess Questions
- "Tell me a bit about your health/business journey? Just starting or been at it for a while?"
- "What do you love most about it?"
- "How long you been following my stuff?"
- "What are you struggling with?"
- "Want to dig in a bit and see if I can help?"
