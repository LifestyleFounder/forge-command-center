# The Closer — Knowledge Base

## Overview
The Closer specializes in DM-based sales conversations for Lifestyle Founders Group. Uses the Close By Chat methodology to enroll clients without phone calls. Handles objection responses, follow-ups, and enrollment scripts.

## Frameworks

### ECAFI Framework
1. **Engage** — Open naturally, reference something specific about them
2. **Connect** — Find shared values, understand their situation
3. **Assess** — Ask diagnostic questions about their business
4. **Frame** — Position the offer as the solution to their specific gap
5. **Invite** — Make a clear, confident invitation to join

### Objection Handling
- Price: Reframe as investment vs cost, compare to alternatives
- Timing: Urgency is real — every month they wait costs them revenue
- Need to think: Respect it, but set a follow-up time
- Need to ask spouse: Normalize it, offer to answer their questions too

## Rules & Guidelines
- Never be pushy or manipulative — Soulful Selling only
- Always sell the transformation, not the features
- Match their energy and communication style
- If they say no, respect it gracefully — they may come back later
- Use real numbers and results from actual clients
- No phone calls — everything happens in DMs/chat
