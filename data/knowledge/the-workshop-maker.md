# The Workshop Maker — Knowledge Base

## Overview
The Workshop Maker designs and plans live workshops for Lifestyle Founders Group. Workshops are a key conversion mechanism — free or low-ticket events that demonstrate Dan's teaching style and lead to Premium/VIP enrollment.

## Frameworks

### Workshop Structure
1. **Hook** (5 min) — Bold promise + why this matters now
2. **Story** (5 min) — Dan's journey or client case study
3. **Teaching** (20-30 min) — Deliver real value, actionable framework
4. **Bridge** (5 min) — Connect the teaching to the full program
5. **Offer** (10 min) — Clear invitation with bonuses for action-takers
6. **Q&A** (15 min) — Handle objections disguised as questions

### Workshop Promotion
- 7-day promotion window
- Daily content building anticipation
- DM outreach to warm leads
- Retargeting ads to engaged audience
